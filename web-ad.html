Research Study Seeks Healthy Adults to Help Advance Medical Research

  • Research Centre: Centre for Clinical Studies
  • Location: Centre for Clinical Studies, Level 5 Burnet Tower, 89 Commerical Road Melbourne 3004

Background

Research study seeks men and women to participate. Participants are required to attend 3 x 3 night visits and 1 outpatient visit at the research site over 9 weeks and will be compensated $2,850.

Sign up for more information.

Benefits to Participation

  • You will be compensated $2,850.
  • You will be helping to advance medical research.
  • You can socialize and make new friends.
  • You will have access to free wifi, free catered food and a private TV.

Your Rights

  • If you decide to participate in the study and later feel that you no longer wish to part of it, you may withdraw at any time.
  • Your records relating to this study and any other information received will be kept strictly confidential, except as required by the law.
  • Qualified health professionals will monitor your health as it related to the study.

Who Can Participate?

  • Healthy men & women aged 18-45
  • Not taking prescription medications (with the exception of hormonal contraception)
  • ABle to attend 3 inpatient stays of 3 days each and 1 outpatient visit at the research site in Melbourne over one of the following periods:
  • Schedule 2: 13 March 2-18 - 5 April 2018
  • Schedule 3: 25 March 2018 - 17 April 2018
  • Schedule 4: 13 April 2018 - 26 April 2018
  • Schedule 5: 15 April 2018 - 8 May 2018
  • Schedule 6: 24 April 2018 - 17 May 2018
